Welcome to Sivmurugaa Restaurant & Rooms, Karur

Discover Sivmurugaa, our versatile outlet on the Karur to Coimbatore Bypass, offering an exquisite South Indian vegetarian dining experience, comfortable rooms, and a spacious function hall for all your events.

Authentic South Indian Vegetarian Cuisine

Sivmurugaa is renowned for its **delicious South Indian vegetarian food**, prepared with authentic recipes and fresh, local ingredients. From aromatic dosas to traditional thalis, our restaurant is a culinary delight for pure vegetarian food lovers in Karur. Perfect for family meals or a quick bite on the go.

Comfortable Rooms for Your Stay

Similar to Aditya Inn, Sivmurugaa also offers a selection of **comfortable and clean rooms** for travelers. Whether you're in Karur for business, pilgrimage, or leisure, our rooms provide a restful environment with essential amenities, ensuring a pleasant stay.

Spacious Function Hall (600 PAX)

Planning an event in Karur? Our **large function hall at Sivmurugaa** can accommodate up to 600 guests (PAX), making it an ideal venue for weddings, corporate meetings, family gatherings, and other special occasions. Equipped with necessary facilities, we ensure your event runs smoothly.

Prime Location on Karur-Coimbatore Bypass

Sivmurugaa is conveniently located on the **Karur to Coimbatore Bypass Road**, near the famous Palamalai Murugan Temple in Pavitram. Its strategic position makes it easily accessible for both local residents and travelers.
