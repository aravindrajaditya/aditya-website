Welcome to Aditya Inn – A Luxurious Stay in Karur

Aditya Inn is a premier 3-star hotel located in the heart of Karur city, just near the Salem ByePass roundana. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or with family, our hotel offers a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and affordability.

About Aditya Inn

Our hotel features beautifully designed Standard and Deluxe rooms, equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, hot water, WiFi, Queen Beds, TVs, and round-the-clock room service. We cater to solo travelers, families, couples, and corporate guests looking for a reliable and restful stay in Karur.

Aditya Inn also features Aditya Mess, a public South Indian restaurant loved by locals and guests alike. We are conveniently located on Salem ByePass Road, close to major business centers, transport hubs, and cultural landmarks of Karur.

Discover Karur City

Karur, located in Tamil Nadu, is known for its vibrant textile industry, cultural heritage, and hospitality. Tourists and professionals alike visit Karur for business meetings, temple tours, and family functions. If you're searching for hotels in Karur or a family stay in Karur, Aditya Inn is your trusted destination.

Our central location makes it easy to access important spots like the Bus Stand, Railway Station, Pasupathieswarar Temple, Karur Vysya Bank headquarters, and various textile companies. Whether you're here for a short stay or long vacation, our amenities and service make your visit memorable.
