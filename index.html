Discover Karur City

Karur, located in Tamil Nadu, is renowned for its **vibrant textile industry**, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Tourists and professionals alike visit Karur for various purposes, including business meetings, sacred temple tours, and family functions. If you're searching for **hotels in Karur**, a comfortable **family stay in Karur**, or a convenient **hotel near Salem Bypass**, Aditya Inn is your trusted destination.

Our central location makes it easy to access important spots like the **Karur Bus Stand**, **Karur Railway Station**, the historic **Pasupathieswarar Temple**, the headquarters of **Karur Vysya Bank**, and numerous textile companies. Whether you're here for a short business trip or a long family vacation, our amenities and dedicated service ensure your visit to Karur is memorable and hassle-free.
