Welcome to Aditya Mess

Aditya Mess is the in-house South Indian restaurant at Aditya Inn, Karur. Open to both our hotel guests and the public, we serve freshly prepared, authentic South Indian dishes every day in a clean and welcoming atmosphere.

Whether you're looking for a hearty breakfast, a wholesome lunch, or a simple dinner, Aditya Mess provides delicious meals made with quality ingredients. We're a popular choice for local diners as well as travelers seeking a family restaurant in Karur.

Why Choose Aditya Mess?

  • Authentic South Indian meals at affordable prices
  • Clean, family-friendly dining space
  • Vegetarian & Non-Vegetarian options
  • Quick service and home-style taste
  • Located within Aditya Inn – easily accessible to everyone

Whether you’re staying with us or just passing by Salem ByePass Road, don’t miss a meal at Aditya Mess – the top-rated family restaurant in Karur.
